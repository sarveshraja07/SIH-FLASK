About the Project

The AI Train Safety Simulation project is designed to maximize train throughput while ensuring passenger safety by preventing collisions on railway tracks. Using Artificial Intelligence, the system continuously monitors train positions, predicts potential collisions, and takes corrective actions by dynamically adjusting train speeds in real-time.

The simulation demonstrates three different scenarios:

  • Without AI: Trains move without intervention, and collisions occur.
  • AI Prediction: The system predicts potential collisions before they happen.
  • AI Prevention: The system actively prevents collisions by reducing train speeds, ensuring a safe distance between trains.

This project highlights how Artificial Intelligence can transform railway systems to be more efficient and safe, supporting both high throughput and reliable passenger travel.
